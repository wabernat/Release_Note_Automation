Supported Operating Environments

Scality S3 Connector 7 is supported on the following operating system distributions (64-bit only) for both storage servers and connectors:

  • RHEL 7.x / CentOS 7.x
  • Ubuntu 14.04 LTS

Severity Labels for Known and Fixed Issues

The following table explains the severity label for each known and fixed issue:

CriticalPresents risk of service outage and/or data loss or corruption
MajorThreatens primary function, performance or stability
MediumCan affect usability, administration or operations
MinorNegligible impact
